Creole burger

Description

Creole Burger is a spicy and flavorful burger inspired by the cuisine of Louisiana. It is made with a seasoned beef patty topped with melted cheese, saut√©ed onions and peppers, and a tangy Creole sauce. The combination of spices and ingredients gives this burger a unique and delicious taste.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons french zesty deli mustard
  • 2 teaspoons creole seasoning, divided
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 hamburger buns, grilled or toasted
  • Slices of tomatoes
  • Slices of onions
  • Lettuce lead

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, mustard, and 1 teaspoon (more if you like things spicier) creole seasoning; set aside

  2. In a larger bowl, mix together the ground beef, worcestershire sauce and 1 teaspoon creole seasoning; combine well.

  3. Shape beef mixture into 4 patties.

  4. Grill or broil patties to desired degree of doneness, turning once.

  5. Spread mustard mixture on split hamburger buns.

  6. On bun bottom, add lettuce, tomato, onion, and patty; cover with bun top.

  7. I dip each bite of my burger in ketchup, too--yummy.
